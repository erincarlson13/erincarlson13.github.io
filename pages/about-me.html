ABOUT ME

Erin graduated with honors from Cornell University in 2011 with a double major in English and Spanish. She promptly surprised everyone she knew--and, most particularly, herself--by accepting an engineering role. Since then, her interests have grown in a more technical direction, but her penchant for spontaneous international travel, morning walks, and warm chocolate croissants remains unchanged.
